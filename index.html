Our Story

Welcome to
Arabian Darbar

Arabian Darbar Restaurant brings the rich taste of Arabian cuisine to the heart of Haripad. Known for flavorful mandi, juicy alfaham, and authentic Arabian dishes, we offer a warm and comfortable dining experience for families, friends, and food lovers.

With carefully prepared spices, tender meats, and perfectly cooked rice, every dish is crafted to deliver the true taste of Arabia — right here in Kerala.
